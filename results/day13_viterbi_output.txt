Connection to Week 1 Project 1:
  Your GC-content scanner used a SLIDING WINDOW approach
  to find AT-rich regions in P. falciparum.
  An HMM-based approach (like this Viterbi demo) would
  instead PROBABILISTICALLY SEGMENT the genome into GC-rich
  and AT-rich states, which is exactly how tools like
  GeneMark and Augustus detect gene boundaries in genomes.
